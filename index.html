Biography

The Wooked Witch is a visionary VJ and live visual artist who combines original live drawings and liquid animations to create immersive, intentional experiences.

Guided by synesthesia and their background in music, they translate sound into flowing, organic visuals. Their unconventional approach combines mixed media into glitch-style animations, creating a fusion of organic and digital distortion.

The Wooked Witch crafts mesmerizing journeys that blend the senses and paint the seams between worlds.
